The central orchestrator of the Cognitive Kernel-Pro system. It does not perform tasks directly but decomposes user queries into plans and delegates them to sub-agents. It maintains a global state including `todo_list`, `completed_list`, and collected `information`.

Key Innovation

The Secret Sauce

High-Quality Data Construction

How do you train an 8B model to beat giants? By curating diverse, verifiable, and complex training trajectories.

Multi-Hop URLQA

To train the agent in complex information retrieval, the team constructed the 'URLQA' dataset. It involves starting from a seed URL and traversing links to aggregate information.

Crucially: The data isn't just Q&A. It includes the full trajectorythe sequence of clicks, scrolls, and reasoning steps.
